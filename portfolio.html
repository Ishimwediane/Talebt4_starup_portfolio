Projects

Industry Monitoring System

This project is designed to monitor industrial environments using various sensors, including temperature sensors and smoke detectors, along with a piezo buzzer and LED indicators. It adjusts its behavior based on day or night settings.

Components used: Temperature sensor, smoke detector, piezo buzzer, LED, Arduino microcontroller.

Challenges and Solutions: During the project, I encountered issues with sensor calibration and signal noise. I resolved these problems by using signal filtering techniques and careful calibration.

Outcome: The system successfully monitors environmental conditions and triggers alarms when thresholds are exceeded, contributing to industrial safety.
